关于我们 | About Us:

MusicTrackHunt is currently just a personal project, born from our passion for discovering and sharing hidden musical gems. We believe good music should never be buried. Every song is personally listened to and carefully handpicked by us, with the hope of bringing you that next “favorite” surprise moment. I'm not a die-hard follower of any single genre, so I keep an open mind toward different styles and languages. I respect all original artists who create with care, and I believe that great songs in less-common languages deserve to be heard as well. What matters most to me is whether the lyrics and arrangement of a song can instantly capture my attention.


For artists: This playlist is an excellent platform to promote your music and reach listeners who are equally passionate about discovering great songs across different styles. You don’t need to spend too much time hesitating or worrying about whether your genre or language fits—what you should care about is the quality of your music. I also won’t dismiss AI-generated music outright, as I've tried out Suno before., and I know that creating a high-quality song with it still takes a great deal of time. If your music is made with Suno, please make sure you've fine-tuned it with care so it meets the basic standards of “good music.”


Currently, the playlist will be capped at no more than 200 songs to ensure that no one’s music gets lost in a massive list of thousands of tracks without a fair chance to be heard.


Please note: Our preferences and interpretation of a song are inevitably influenced by our personal, subjective feelings. Therefore, if your music is not selected by us, it does not mean we are denying its value. You are welcome to submit it to other playlists, and I believe you will find listeners who appreciate it.
